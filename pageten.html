The Statue Chamber:

As you cross on through the water gates, you've entered a large chamber, but this isn't an ordinary chamber.
You feel nothing but an overwhelming pressure in the air when you step into the chamber.
What you see in front of you are 5 large foreboding statues that show winged beings carrying weapons, except one as it seems to be partially destroyed.
Of the 5 statues, only 4 have placards that state the names of these statues. Michael, Gabriel, Raphael, and on the destroyed statue, Lucifer.
One for some reason doesn't have a name on it, but the statue carries a large sword.
You have this feeling that you need to leave the room, but you feel there is more to explore.

What decision shall you make?
