The Archangel Blade:

You decided to investigate the rest of the chamber.
You decided to take a closer look at the statue that did not have a placard for its name. As you inspected the statue, you noticed an indentation of where the placard should be.
You found a small space within and pushed a button. From the statue's chest, a blade pops out. The blade in question has a drill like design with the handle wrapped in leather. A note that was on it states it is an Archangel blade.
You wonder why it was hidden in the statue. But you feel you have a connection to this blade.

Who does this blade belong to, and why do you have a connection to it?
