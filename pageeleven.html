The Axial Mundi:

When you crossed on through the door, the entire scenery changed. What you see is a long road ahead.
The scenery looks as if you're back on earth, but you also know this doesn't feel like home, so it must mean you're still in heaven.
You then hear this loud screeching noise in your head as if some is trying to speak to you, but you can somehow clearly make out what it is saying.
“Follow the Axial Mundi, follow the path” the voice said. Is this what it is called.
Is as if you have no choice but to follow this path, but you feel this voice is trying to mislead you.

What decision shall you make?
