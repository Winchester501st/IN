Through the Pearly Gates:

As you walk through the pearly gates, what stands before you are a long pearly hallway.
As you walk through the hallway, you've noticed all the doors seem to have some placards on them that detail what they are.
One door indicates a birth and death statistics office, another states it is a grand library.
You've also noticed that it seems like you are being followed. Is someone keeping track of you for a reason?
Nevertheless, you are unable to do anything about it since you're unarmed.
You now come across two doors that stand out from the rest, a door that is full of white feathers and another with seal on it that shows winged extended out.

Which path shall you take?
