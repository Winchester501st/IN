About the Author:

Bryan Gonzalez is currently a student a the University of Central Florida, where he is currently studying for Digital Media-Game Design track.
Growing up, Bryan has been known to play many games throughout his life. Some of his favorite titles include Persona 5, God of War (2018), God of War Ragnarok, Bloodborne, Spider-man, and Final Fantasy XVI

Outside of College, Bryan is currently serving in the United States Marine Corps Reserve, and is employed with the Walt Disney Company, where he is creating magic for guest as a smuggler at Millenium Falcon Smuggler's Run
