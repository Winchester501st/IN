Metatron:

As you try to leave the portal chamber, you encounter a blinding light.
You feel a foreboding presence in front of you. “Well done human, you have passed this test and ignored your curiosity. I am Metatron, and I will be taking you with me” this presence in front of you said.
The blinding light fades away and you get a better view of Metatron. He looks to be robotic and chromed out, with mechanical wings.
You asked why he is taking you. Metatron says, “So you can help end this war and achieve my visions”.

You wonder if this is the right choice you've made?
