Banished:

As you entered the portal, you were then transported to another location. You then wake up on the ground, but your surroundings feel different.
Are you back on Earth? So, it would seem since you don't see the silver city no more. All you see is a war-torn Earth.
You then hear a voice,” You fool, did you not read. You have allowed your curiosity to take the better of you.”
You didn't understand what was going on. “For your foolishness, you have been banished from Heaven, as decreed by Metatron” the voice says.
You are left to wonder what will happen next.

Did you make the right choice?
