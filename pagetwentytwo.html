The Seraph's arrived:

As you try to turn back around, a group of winged beings approaches you. You recognize right away that they are angels.
“We have been watching you, abomination, you are to come with us” the angels said.
Why do I need to go with them, and why am I an Abomination.
“In order to win this war against Hell, you are here to help us, but we will decide your fate after the war, for you are a Nephilim, an abomination of Heaven”.
A Nephilim, what is that? Was I never human this entire time? Just who were my parents?

You have no choice but to go with them, but you wonder what other choices you were to have made differently?
