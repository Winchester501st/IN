The Garden:

You continued the path, and once more the scenery changed. This time, your scenery looks like to be a beautiful garden.
You have made it to your destination it seems like.
You also remember this garden from back home. It is a garden you visited while in grade school.
You encountered what look like to be angel that has 12 wings! 6 of them being white while the other 6 black.
“You've made it my son” the angel said.
As you stepped closer, you realized it's your father! But how could that be? What about mother? Was she human?
“Son, I'm here to train you to fight against both heaven and hell, for it is time for them to end. You are the reason I left Heaven so I could protect you.”

You made it to the Garden, but what over choices were there before getting here?
