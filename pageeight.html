The Portal Chamber:

As you walked through the silver gate, you seemed to have entered another chamber, but different from the previous chamber you've witnessed.
What you see before you are a bunch of portals that lead to somewhere. Where do they lead?
And why are there portals in heaven? Wouldn't one assume these creatures called angels be able to teleport anywhere they want.
Nonetheless, you have this feeling that you shouldn't be in here in the first place.
You decided to investigate these portals in closer detail, and you noticed that there are placards nearby that have writing on them.
The writing that you couldn't understand earlier now seems to have partial legibility that you could potentially make out what it's saying.
It seems to say, “Only the Archangels have permission to use these portals”.

Do you go through the portal or leave?
